Googley Choice Pamphlet

Tre Yearwood


This project shows the abilities I have in Canva. I am capable of making a pamphlet designed to represent a company and design it to attract the attention of users and business partners alike. My pamphlet includes a company address, company policies, a contact feature, company phone number, etc. I choose an appropriate color theme that would be unique to readers but not a headache to look at. I use many different other features to give the readers an incentive to read my pamphlet. This is just one example of the many possibilities I have to offer with Canva.
