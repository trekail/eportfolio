With the use of a website called “GoDaddy” I have made a website to represent a company named The Googley Choice which specializes with electronic devices. Using this I made an ecommerce website including the usual features such as the history of the company, a store, charity, policies, etc. I put my interests also into designing the website to attract customers, potential employees, and other companies that may want to interact with The Googley Choice. I am able to put the companies desires, or interests first while making the website and refine it to make the website the best it can be.
